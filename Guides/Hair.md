# Hair

A tight scalp chokes off the blood supply your follicles need to grow hair, so every night massage your scalp until it moves. Put your whole hand flat against your head and push the scalp around: front, back, and both sides, gripping and pulling up with your palm so the skin shifts over the bone. Test it by pinching: healthy scalp lifts off the skull and feels flexible. A mobile scalp lets blood flow to the surface, and that flow is what feeds growth. Do it every single night.
