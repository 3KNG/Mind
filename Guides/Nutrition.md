# Nutrition

**Two lists. That's the whole diet.**

### ✅ EAT (these fill you up)
**Protein. Fat. Fiber.**
Meat, eggs, fish, cheese, butter, oil, avocado, nuts, leafy greens.

### ❌ SKIP (these never fill you up)
**Sugar + refined carbs.**
Bread, cereal, chips, sweets, anything with the fiber stripped out.

---

**The mantra:**
> Protein, fiber, fat fill you up. Sugar empties you out.

**The one question for any food:**
> Is the sugar stripped out, or packed in?

*Keto: fat is your fuel. Sugar is the only enemy.*
