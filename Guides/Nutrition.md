# Nutrition

Eat protein, fat, and fiber and you fill up fast and stop on your own; eat sugar and refined carbs and you never feel full, so you keep going. So live off meat, eggs, fish, cheese, butter, oil, avocado, nuts, and leafy greens, and skip bread, cereal, chips, sweets, and anything with the fiber stripped out. The only question for any food is whether the sugar is stripped out or packed in. On keto, fat is your fuel and sugar is the only enemy.
